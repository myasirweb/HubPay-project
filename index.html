Products & Services (EMI)

Hubpay Wallet

Hubpay Wallet is a standard wallet account that can be operated through our “Mobile Application” and “Website portal”. You can sign-up for the wallet using our instant and simple onboarding process that allows you the freedom to transact up to an aggregate monthly load limit of Rs. 50,000.

Hub Plus Wallet

Hubpay Plus offers an enhanced wallet account with aggregate monthly limits of up to Rs. 200,000 (or Rs. 400,000) after performing an additional biometric verification. Hubpay Wallet users can upgrade to Hub Plus, to have more control over their financial services’ needs.

Hubpay Plus (Enhanced)

Existing hubpay users may upgrade their wallet accounts to Hubpay Plus (Enhanced) to enjoy increased transactional limits through our application or web-portal by following easy steps.

Hubpay Minor Wallet

Minors, aged 14-17 years, can open a wallet account with access to almost all the features available to adult bank account holders, simply by following a straightforward and seamless signup process. This allows you to enjoy full control of your pocket money and monitor your spending

Hub Plus Minor Wallet

Minors aged 14-17 years, who work as freelancers, can sign up for our distinctive ABC Plus Minor Account. This account offers innovative features designed to facilitate hassle-free payment receipts for young freelancers.

Merchant Wallets

Hubpay offers innovative and efficient payment acceptance methods for merchants and business with a seamless onboarding using simplified KYC and due diligence. The payment acceptance channels range from QR codes, Till Payments, Gateways etc., depending on merchant and business needs and requirements

What is Hubpay?

Hubpay Pakistan is a State Bank of Pakistan’s Regulated Entity (RE), currently holding an Inprinciple Approval for an Electronic Money Institution. Hubpay Pakistan is a registered private company with SECP with CUI: 0179726 and NTN: 4988569
+

What are Hubpay Pakistan’s product and service offerings?

Hubpay Pakistan offers innovative financial solutions for all your business and personal needs, including digital wallets, financial wellness solutions, collection and merchant payments services etc.
+

What are the account limits of different wallet accounts offered by Hubpay?

Each Hubpay wallet offers arying account limits based on the level of due diligence performed. For more detailed guidance on account limits, please visit the 'Products and Services' section.
+

How do I fund my Wallet?

You can initially fund your wallet through an Inter-Bank Fund Transfer (IBFT) from your account with another bank or by receiving an Inter-Bank Fund Transfer (IBFT) or a Wallet-to-Wallet Transfer from someone else.
+

How do I make the payments from my wallet?

You can initiate wallet-to-wallet transfers, IBFTs (Interbank Fund Transfers), bill payments, and mobile top-ups up to the available balance in your wallet account
